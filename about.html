About MoodShelf

MoodShelf is a simple, intuitive tool designed for readers who choose their next book based on how they feel. Instead of forcing yourself into a rigid reading list, MoodShelf helps you flow with your emotions and pick books that resonate with your mood at the moment.

Why MoodShelf Exists

Many readers find that their enjoyment of a book depends heavily on their emotional state. You may crave a cozy escape one day and something intense or thought-provoking the next. MoodShelf embraces this idea by letting you:

  • Check in with your mood before reading
  • Discover book suggestions that match how you feel
  • Build a personal shelf of your current and future reads
  • Track how your reading habits shift over time

It’s an experience built on the idea that reading is personal — and your mood is part of the story.

What MoodShelf Offers

MoodShelf is continuously evolving, but its core features include:

  • Check-In: Reflect on your current emotional state.
  • Discover: Explore books based on mood and reading energy.
  • My Shelf: Save and organize your reading list.
  • Lightweight & Private: No forced accounts, no unnecessary tracking.

The goal is to help you read more intentionally — not more rigidly.
